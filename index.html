Leigh-on-Sea Board Game Club

Free weekly meetup to play board games of all types

Meetup dedicated to those who want to play board games in person


Find it difficult to find people to play board games with? So did we! But not anymore. There's no need to force games on your friends here. No need to convince your partner into sympathy games. This is a meet up entirely attended by people who want to play board games in person and it's wonderful!
