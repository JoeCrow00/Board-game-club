Leigh-on-Sea Board Game Club

Free weekly meetup to play board games of all types

A group dedicated to those who want to play board games in person


Find it difficult to find people to play board games with? So did we! But not anymore. There's no need to force games on your friends here. No need to convince your partner to play with you out of sympathy. This is a meet up entirely attended by people who want to play board games in person. Yep.
